## Second Cursor Meetup in Bali — June 3, 2026

Highlights from our second Cursor Meetup in Bali.

This meetup was intentionally designed for beginners, and we had a fantastic mix of attendees — from aspiring developers and beginner coders to professionals in marketing, design, and product management. Keeping the group small allowed us to provide hands-on support and help everyone get set up and comfortable with Cursor.

---

### What the evening featured

- An introduction to Cursor session that received great feedback
- Two community lightning talks showcasing real-world workflows and projects
- Plenty of discussions, learning, and collaborations

The most exciting outcome was seeing how motivated everyone was after the event. Attendees left eager to continue exploring Cursor, build projects, and join future meetups with more questions, ideas, and experiences to share.

It's incredibly rewarding to watch this community grow and evolve. With every meetup, we're improving the experience, refining the format, and helping more people discover the power of Cursor.
