Living in Bali is easier and more meaningful when you can speak a bit of the local language. Even simple phrases can change how you connect with people, from ordering coffee to having real conversations with locals.

<!--more-->

That's why BaliSquad is hosting regular **free Indonesian language class** at **Bukithub Coworking**.

## What this class is about

This is a beginner-friendly, practical Indonesian language class focused on:

- Everyday phrases
- Common expressions you actually use
- Confidence speaking, not grammar perfection

## Who this class is for

This class is open to:

- Newcomers to Bali  
- Expats who want to connect better locally  
- Digital nomads and remote workers  
- Anyone curious about learning Indonesian

You don't need to be a student, founder, or coworking member.

---

## What you will learn

Instead of rigid grammar rules, we focus on:

- Greetings and introductions
- Ordering food and drinks
- Asking for directions
- Making small talk
- Shopping and negotiating

The goal is to get you speaking from day one.

---

## Why it matters

Learning Indonesian helps you:

- Build relationships with locals
- Navigate daily life more easily
- Show respect for the culture you're living in
- Get better prices and service
- Feel more at home in Bali
