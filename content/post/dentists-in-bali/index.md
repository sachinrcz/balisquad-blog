Every second person on the planet fears going to the dentist. Adults especially feel this fear more than children — we all have strong childhood memories of the dentist.

Whether you like it or not, finding the right dentist matters. To help ensure you never skip a check-up again, we examined the most popular dental clinics on Bali.

<!--more-->

> **Note:** Prices are not fixed and final costs depend on the complexity of the case and type of treatment. Every clinic has both positive and negative reviews — this depends on specific situations. English-speaking staff are available in all clinics, although communication can sometimes be difficult.

---

## Canggu & Seminyak

### Bali 911 Dental Clinic

**Location:** Central Seminyak  
**Hours:** 12:00–17:00 daily (closed Sunday)

A team of highly qualified dentists offering a wide range of dental services, including preventative, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry. They use modern equipment to provide high-level care. The staff are friendly and welcoming.

**Sample Prices:**

- Cleaning – IDR 600,000  
- Tooth restoration – IDR 600,000–1,000,000  
- Root canal treatment – IDR 1,000,000  
- Porcelain-metal crown – IDR 5,000,000  
- Teeth whitening – IDR 4,000,000  
- At-home whitening (incl. cleaning) – IDR 3,100,000  
- Implants – IDR 22,000,000–25,000,000

---

### Pearl Dental Studio

**Hours:** 09:00–19:00 daily (closed Sunday)

Specializes in dental implants and cosmetic dentistry, with experience in general treatments too. Known for quality and attention to detail.

**Sample Prices:**

- Composite filling – IDR 800,000  
- Tooth extraction – IDR 2,000,000  
- Regular cleaning – IDR 800,000  
- Whitening – IDR 4,500,000  
- Initial consultation – IDR 250,000

---

### Sunset Dental Bali

**Hours:** 09:00–20:00 daily (closed Friday & Sunday)

Offers general, cosmetic, and orthodontic dentistry with modern equipment, digital X-rays, and intraoral cameras. Known for professional and friendly service.

**Prices:**

- Consultation – Free  
- Panoramic X-ray – Free  
- Periapical X-ray (endo) – IDR 250,000  
- Laser whitening (90 min) – IDR 3,500,000

---

### Dokter Gigi | Giqu Dental Canggu

**Hours:** 11:00–20:00 daily (closed Sunday)

Modern clinic with a range of services including preventive, restorative, and orthodontic care. The staff are professional, and many praise Dr. Adinda for her English and skill.

**Sample Prices:**

- Whitening – IDR 2,200,000–2,500,000  
- Fillings – IDR 525,000–750,000  
- Porcelain crown – from IDR 4,000,000  
- Tooth extraction – from IDR 300,000  
- Composite veneer – IDR 800,000  
- Metal braces – IDR 4,000,000–7,000,000

---

### Permata Dental Bali

**Hours:** 09:00–21:00 daily

Provides popular services — whitening, veneers, operations, etc. They often have on-site promotions and 15% discounts for children. Book early due to high demand.

**Sample Prices:**

- Cleaning – IDR 200,000–300,000  
- Tooth extraction – IDR 250,000–750,000  
- Porcelain veneers – from IDR 2,500,000  
- Braces – from IDR 4,000,000

## Denpasar

### Dentes Dental Clinic (Klinik Gigi DENTES)

**Hours:** 09:00–21:00 (closed Sunday)

A small clinic popular with expats due to affordable prices and competent care. Offers implants, general dentistry, braces, crowns, bridges, and more.

**Prices:**

- Consultation – IDR 150,000  
- Filling – IDR 380,000–550,000  
- Canal cleaning – IDR 780,000  
- Simple extraction – IDR 300,000–450,000  
- Surgical extraction – IDR 2,000,000–2,300,000

---

### My Dentist Bali

**Hours:** Mon–Sat 09:00–19:30

A private practice opened by Dr. Ignasius Yonas. Clean and cozy with positive reviews; some mixed feedback on crowns.

**Typical Prices:**

- Consultation – IDR 300,000  
- X-rays – IDR 450,000–1,250,000  
- Tooth extraction – IDR 1,000,000–6,000,000
