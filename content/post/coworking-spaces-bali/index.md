Coworking spaces have become the standard work format for digital nomads and remote professionals. They suit those who don't want to work in isolation and value live professional surroundings.

<!--more-->

The home office format isn't always convenient: it's hard to concentrate and separate work from personal life. Coworking spaces offer a more structured environment — with workstations, stable internet, and conditions for focus.

Beyond infrastructure, these spaces build community. There are many coworking spaces on Bali, but the level of comfort and atmosphere vary noticeably — let's explore further.

---

## Bukit Area

### BukitHub Coworking

**Location:** Uluwatu, quiet area near residential neighborhoods and cafes  
**Hours:** Daily, 24/7

**Internet:** Stable fast Wi-Fi

**Description**

A small coworking space that started as an office for two people and eventually grew into a full-fledged workspace. Suitable for those looking for a calm work environment with basic but thoughtful infrastructure.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

Simple layout: wood, light walls, lots of greenery. There are shared tables and separate zones for quiet work. Lighting is soft, noise level is minimal. The atmosphere is calm, without the feeling of office strictness.

**Work Amenities**

- Stable internet
- 24/7 access
- Meeting rooms with screens
- Monitors, keyboards, 3D printer
- Game zone (board games, PlayStation)
- Kitchen with free coffee, tea, and water
- Small outdoor zone

**Community**

Active and friendly. They hold lectures, workshops, language meetups, and game nights. New residents quickly integrate into the community.

Bukit Hub has a good collection of board games to unwind, and residents receive discounts at cafes, sports studios, and local services — a nice bonus to workdays in Uluwatu.

**Pricing**

- Day pass — 150K IDR
- Week pass — 600K IDR  
- Month pass — 2M IDR

Virtual office and hourly meeting room rentals available.

### Uluwatu Hub

**Location:** Uluwatu, green area with open terraces and pools  
**Hours:** Daily, 24/7

**Internet:** Up to 1.5 Gbps + backup channel

**Description**

A calm coworking space in Balinese style, focused on focused work. Suitable for those who want stable internet, silence, and the ability to work in a comfortable environment close to nature.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

Open spaces, terraces, lots of air and greenery. Inside — air-conditioned quiet rooms and comfortable workstations. The atmosphere encourages concentration and a measured work rhythm.

**Work Amenities**

- Shared and individual workstations
- Meeting rooms with projectors
- Focus rooms and phone booths
- 24/7 card access
- Lockers
- Printing and scanning by quota
- Two swimming pools
- Parking for bikes and cars
- Amaranth restaurant (8:00–22:00)
- Room and villa rentals on-site

**Community**

Freelancers, startup founders, and remote professionals. Regular masterclasses, workshops, meetups, and small poolside events.

**Pricing**

- Day pass — 200K IDR (10 hours, drink, food discounts, pool access)
- 24-hour pass — 250K IDR (24 hours, up to 3 devices, drink, food discounts, pool access)

### Monday Coworking and Coffee

**Location:** Ungasan, Uluwatu. Quiet street near the golf course, away from main traffic.  
**Hours:** Mon–Sat: 08:00–23:00, Sun: Closed

**Internet:** Average around 200 Mbps

**Description**

Monday is a coworking space with a cafe, focused on calm and concentrated work. Suitable for those looking for a comfortable space with good internet and the ability to work in silence without dropping out of communication.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

The main work zone is designed in calm dark tones with soft lighting. The space looks cozy and intimate, with plants throughout the coworking space. Meeting zones are lighter and simpler in design — minimalist furniture and windows overlooking the garden. The interior is neat and functional, without unnecessary details.

**Work Amenities**

- Large shared tables
- Small cabins for 2–4 people
- Phone booths
- Meeting rooms
- Photo and podcast recording studio
- Workstations near large windows
- Indoor and outdoor zones
- Cafe zone and garden with seating

**Community**

The Monday team places great emphasis on community: meetups, creative activities, quiet internal evenings, and sports activities. Including — a padel club that gathers participants every week. This is a place where it's easy to meet people and feel part of a common rhythm.

**Pricing**

- Daily, weekly, and monthly passes
- Meeting rooms — from 150K IDR/hour

Current prices and promotions — on their website and Instagram.

## Jimbaran Area

### Jimbaran Hub

**Hours:** Mon–Sat: 8:00–22:00, Sun: 8:00–18:00

Jimbaran Hub is a large creative space where work naturally blends with daily life. The same territory houses offices, studios, sports facilities, children's programs, art halls, a padel club, go-karting, a skate park, and a cafe. Throughout the day, there are meetings, classes, masterclasses, exhibitions, concerts, and small festivals. This is a place where people come not only to work but also to spend time, meet, and participate in events.

Two coworking spaces operate within the complex — JLoft and Colabo.

#### JLoft (Jimbaran Hub)

**Hours:** Mon–Fri, 09:00–18:00

**Internet:** Fast stable Wi-Fi

**Description**

JLoft is a more office-like and structured coworking format. Suitable for focused work, team meetings, negotiations, and those who need a private office or calm work atmosphere.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

Light halls without visual noise, long work tables, comfortable chairs, and plenty of power outlets. There's a separate meeting room with large windows, a glass table, and an interactive panel for presentations.

**Work Amenities**

- Open space workstations
- Private offices of various sizes
- Fast internet and air conditioning
- Meeting room with 58″ touch screen, sound, and microphones
- Ability to order food for meetings
- Access to all Jimbaran Hub infrastructure (Colabo cafe, lounge zones, sports facilities, parking)

#### Colabo Coworking (Jimbaran Hub)

**Hours:** Daily, 09:00–17:00

**Description**

Colabo is a more lively and creative workspace. Suitable for those who prefer an informal atmosphere and working in a dynamic environment.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

Indoor and semi-outdoor zones, lots of plants, soft sofas, and cozy corners. The space is filled with movement: people work, communicate, and participate in events.

**Work Amenities**

- Indoor and outdoor workstations
- Meeting cabins and small quiet rooms
- Colabo cafe
- Lounge zones
- Air conditioning
- Regular community events

## Bingin Area

### The Space Bali

**Location:** Bingin, green territory near the beach  
**Hours:** Mon–Sun 07:00–23:00

**Internet:** 125.4 Mbps

**Description**

The Space Bali is a coworking and community where work, yoga, and socializing exist in one rhythm. It's convenient to spend a whole day here: start the morning with practice, work in the shade of palm trees, have lunch at the cafe, and stay for an evening class or meetup. The space is immersed in greenery and creates the feeling of a small oasis where it's easy to combine productivity and restoration.

**Atmosphere and Interior**

Lots of greenery, open zones, calm pace, and warm, friendly atmosphere. The space encourages communication and easily helps integrate into the community.

**Work Amenities**

- Coworking with comfortable chairs and fast Wi-Fi
- Office services
- Outdoor zones for work and relaxation
- Cafe for snacks and meetings
- Regular yoga and pilates classes
- Teacher Training 200HR

**Community**

Active and warm. It's easy to meet people, discuss ideas, and find like-minded individuals — many come not only to work but also to be part of the space.

**Pricing**

- Single visit — 226K IDR
- After 4:00 PM — 113K IDR
- Coworking + yoga — 283K IDR
- Memberships: from 3 visits — 447K IDR, unlimited month — 2,712K IDR
- Packages for 15 / 30 / 50 / 100 hours and memberships for 3 months
